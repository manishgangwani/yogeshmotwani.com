About Yogesh Motwani

Yogesh is a passionate user experience designer with over 3 years of experience in the enterprise technology space. Currently working at Guidewire, he focuses on design solutions for underwriters in the insurance industry. He comes from a computer engineering background, which helps him to collaborate better with the product engineering teams.

As a UX Designer he thrives for user centered solutions and strongly believes that a complex product doesn't need to have a complex experience.
