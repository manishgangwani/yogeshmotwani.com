ABOUT

Yogesh is currently working at SAP Labs as a UI/UX Designer after completing his graduation with Masters in Interactive Digital Media from Northeastern University. Prior to working at SAP, he was working at Northeastern University as a UI Designer and Prototyper.

Before pursing his Masters, Yogesh had worked at Loginext Solutions as a UI/UX Designer and Developer Intern. His skills are not limited to identifying problems, quantitative and qualitative analysis, developing persona, designing, and performing usability testing, but he is also skilled in developing interactive prototypes using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery. He continues to explore trends and keep up-to-date focus in industry news, technologies, and design methodologies.
